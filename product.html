Carfax + YCNC

You already have the history.
Now get what it means.

A Carfax report is one of the most powerful documents in a used-car transaction. It tells you what happened to the car — every accident, every owner, every odometer reading on record. What it cannot tell you is what any of it means for the specific machine in front of you, what it will cost, or whether you should buy it.

That interpretive layer is what Yes Car No Car provides. And when you bring both together, the verdict doesn't just get better. It gets precise.

"Drop in your Carfax and the verdict only gets sharper."

A vehicle's accident history means different things depending on where the impact occurred, what the car is, and what systems were in range. Regional history matters too — a car that spent years in a flood-prone area or near coastal salt carries risk that no accident report surfaces on its own. A single long-term owner at high mileage tells a different story than four owners in seven years at the same number.

Yes Car No Car reads all of it. Not as isolated data points — as a complete picture of what this car is, what it has been through, and what is likely coming next.
