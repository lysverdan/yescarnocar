How It Works

Three steps. Built for buyers and owners.

Whether you're considering a purchase, verifying a deal, or simply trying to understand the car you already own — Yes Car No Car works the same way.

Scan & enter the basics

Point your phone at any windshield, dashboard, or door jamb — or type the VIN in. Add the asking price and mileage. Have a Carfax® report? Upload it — the verdict gets even sharper.

Get the verdict

The verdict is free — one of four, on any car. The full report goes deeper: history, recalls, market value, three-year cost projection, upcoming maintenance.

Ask anything

Follow up with questions specific to your situation. Stored against the VIN forever — accessible anytime you need it.
