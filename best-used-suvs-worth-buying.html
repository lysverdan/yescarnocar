What Are the Best Used SUVs Worth Buying?

Published July 22, 2026 · Updated July 22, 2026

Every list of the best used SUVs tells you that the Toyota RAV4 and Honda CR-V are reliable. That is true, but it stops exactly where the useful question begins.

Quick answer

The best used SUVs are generally the Toyota RAV4, Honda CR-V, Mazda CX-5, Subaru Forester, Subaru Outback, and Subaru Crosstrek. The right choice depends on whether the model's expensive risks can be inspected, priced, and negotiated before purchase.

A good SUV is not one that never breaks. It is one where the most likely expensive problem is something you can see coming, test for, price out, and either fix or negotiate before you buy. These are the used SUVs that earn favorable verdicts — and the specific conditions that still matter.

The safest compact SUV choices

Toyota RAV4

The RAV4 earns its reputation through proven powertrains, huge parts availability, reasonable repair costs, and strong resale value. On many used examples, the bigger concerns are ordinary wear: brakes, tires, suspension, neglected fluids, and collision repairs.

The pattern worth remembering

The best used SUVs are not the ones with zero known issues. They are the ones whose costly issues are discoverable before purchase: a CVT you can test for shudder, an oil-consumption pattern you can investigate, a timing component you can confirm by records, or a windshield cost you can price in advance.

The dangerous vehicles are the ones with systemic failures that cannot be reliably ruled out through inspection, service history, or configuration.

Why Yes Car No Car may disagree with other SUV rankings

Most rankings treat any known issue as a negative. We separate manageable, inspectable risk from systemic risk. That is why a Forester, Outback, or Audi Q3 can earn Good Car — With Conditions instead of a simplistic yes or no.

The bottom line

The best used SUV is not one perfect model. It is a RAV4, CR-V, CX-5, Forester, Outback, Crosstrek — or the right Q3 — bought correctly. Pick the platform that fits your life, identify the one or two expensive risks that matter, inspect for them, and negotiate everything else.

Buy the SUV whose problems you can see. That is the whole secret.

Frequently asked questions

What is the most reliable used SUV?

The Toyota RAV4 and Honda CR-V are the safest general answers, with the Mazda CX-5 close behind. The exact best choice depends on model year, engine, maintenance, and condition.

Is a used Subaru SUV reliable?

Many Foresters, Outbacks, and Crosstreks are good purchases when oil consumption, CVT condition, service history, and rust are checked before buying.

Should I buy a used luxury SUV?

Only when the exact model's expensive failure points are known, inspectable, and supported by records. A cheap luxury SUV without documentation can become very expensive quickly.

What should I inspect on a used SUV?

Check the drivetrain, transmission behavior, oil use, cooling system, suspension, tires, rust, accident repairs, recalls, all-wheel-drive service, and maintenance history.
